Nepal

List of Cities

  • Kathmandu
    • The capital and also the largest city with a population of 1,442,271!
  • Pokhara
    • The largest metropolitan city covering 464.28km²!
  • Bhaktapur
    • Known as the city of temples, has the best-preserved palace courtyards in Nepal

List of Landmarks

  • Mount Everest
    • On the border of Nepal and China lies the worlds highest mountain!
  • Lumbini
    • Lumbini houses the Maya Devi temple and its Sacred Garden, known as the birthplace of the Shakyamuni Buddha
  • Chitwan National Park
    • Approximately 930², this park provides protected land for the Royal Bengal tigers, rhinoceros and over 500 bird species

List of Famous Citizens

  • Guatama Buddha
      Lived in Nepal between the 6th and 4th century B.C., known as the founder of Bhuddism!
  • Tenzing Norgay
    • A Sherpa mountaineer, holds the honor of being one of the first two individuals to reach the summit of Mount Everest
  • Jhamak Ghimire
    • Despite having cerebral palsy, she became one of the most celebrated poets of Nepal!
