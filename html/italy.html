Italy
Cities
  • Rome

    Existing for almost 3,000 years, Rome stands as Italy's capital and most populated city!

  • Venice

    Venice is located in a lagoon in the Adriatic sea, built on more than 100 small islands! The city has no roads, just canals including the Grand Canal.

  • Florence

    Florence is located in the Tuscany region of Italy. It is home to many works of Renaissance art and architecture.

  • Milan

    Milan is the global capital of fashion and design. It also is home to the national stock exchange!

    • Landmarks
  • The Colosseum

    First opening in 80 AD, the Colosseum is the largest amphitheatre ever built.

  • Leaning Tower of Pisa

    The tower is a freestanding standing bell tower best known for its nearly 4-degree lean!

  • Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore

    Known as the Cathedral of Florence, its constuction took almost 150 years to build!

    • Famous Citizens
  • Leonardo da Vinci

    Best known for his paintings the Mona Lisa and the Last Supper, da Vinci lived from 1452-1519.

  • Andrea Bocelli

    Bocelli is a well known opera singer who was blinded at 12 years old from a football accident.

  • Pope Pietro Francesco Orsini

    Orsini was head of the Catholic Church from 1724 until his death in 1730. Fun fact, he is a distant cousin of mine!
